Common Symptoms

Anxiety affects both the mind and body.

  • Chest tightness
  • Fast heartbeat
  • Sweating or trembling
  • Stomach discomfort
  • Feeling unsafe or on edge

How to Manage Anxiety

Anxiety is highly treatable with the right tools.

  • Breathing exercises
  • Grounding techniques
  • Regular exercise
  • Talking to a therapist
